Udon Noodle Soup

This is my go-to recipe for when I crave Japanese food. It is very easy to make and the ingredients are simple. Perfect for cold weather as well!

Ingredients

  • 1 package frozen udon noodles
  • 500 ml kombu dashi
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 1 tbsp mirin
  • 1 tbsp sake
  • for garnish: tofu, spring onions, togarashi, etc.

Steps

  1. Put the kombu dashi in a pot and bring to a simmer.
  2. Add the soy sauce, mirin, and sake. Cook for 5 minutes in low heat.
  3. Meanwhile, cook the frozen udon noodles as per the package instructions. Drain.
  4. To assemble, put udon in a bowl and pour the soup. Add whatever toppings you like!
