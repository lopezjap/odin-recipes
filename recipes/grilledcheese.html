Grilled Cheese Sandwich

This classic snack is very easy to make for lunch or even as a midnight treat!

Ingredients

  • 1 tablespoon mayonnaise
  • 2 slices toast bread
  • 2 slices cheddar cheese
  • a pinch of pepper
  • ketchup

Steps

  1. Heat up a pan on medium heat. Spread mayonnaise on one side of the toast bread. Place bread on the pan with mayonnaise side down.
  2. Put the slices of cheese and pepper. Spread mayonnaise on the other toast and put it mayonnaise side top.
  3. Cook until lightly browned on one side. Flip over and cook until other side is brown and cheese has melted.
  4. Serve with ketchup as a dip.
