Kimchi Pancake

If you have kimchi that needs to be used up, this is the perfect meal to make! The recipe if pretty flexible and one can add more chilis or vegetables.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup kimchi
  • 1/2 cup flour
  • 1/4 cup water
  • 4 tablespoons kimchi juice
  • 2 tablespoons oil
  • for the dipping sauce: soy sauce, rice venegar, sugar, and gochugaru

Steps

  1. Put the kimchi, flour, water, and kimchi juice in a bowl. Mix well together.
  2. Heat up the oil in a pan with medium high heat.
  3. Pour the batter and cook until the edges are cooked, about 3-4 minutes.
  4. Flip and cook until the other side is crispy. Transfer to a plate.
  5. Mix 1 tablespoon each of soy sauce, rice venegar, sugar and gochugaru. Serve dip while kimchi pancake is hot and crispy!
